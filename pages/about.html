Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers! Learn everything you need to plan your trip with MonksTrip and enjoy a hassle-free journey.

How do I book a trip with MonksTrip? add_circle_outline

You can easily book a trip by browsing our Destinations or Services pages and clicking "Book Now". Alternatively, reach out to us via the Contact page for a fully personalized booking.

Can I customize my travel itinerary? remove_circle_outline

Absolutely! We offer personalized itineraries to match your preferences. Just let us know your travel needs, and we'll take care of the rest.

Is MonksTrip eco-friendly? add_circle_outline

Yes. We are committed to sustainable travel and partner with eco-conscious accommodations and transport providers to minimize our environmental footprint.

What happens if I need to cancel or reschedule? add_circle_outline

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the package. However, we strive to offer flexible rescheduling options up to 30 days before departure. Please refer to our detailed terms during booking.

Do you provide travel insurance? add_circle_outline

Travel insurance is highly recommended. While it's not included by default in all packages, we offer comprehensive add-on coverage options during the checkout process.
