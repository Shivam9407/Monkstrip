The Journey

Day 01

Arrival & Oia Golden Hour

Arrive at Santorini's Thira airport where your private chauffeur awaits. Check into your boutique cliffside suite. As the afternoon wanes, enjoy a private guided walk through the winding streets of Oia.

  • check_circle Private Transfer
  • check_circle Luxury Suite Check-in
  • check_circle Gourmet Sunset Dinner
Day 02

The Caldera Sails

Board a luxury catamaran for a private cruise around the volcanic caldera. Swim in the thermal hot springs and explore hidden coves. A Mediterranean lunch is prepared on board.

  • check_circle 5-Hour Private Cruise
  • check_circle Hot Spring Bathing
  • check_circle Seafood Lunch on Deck
Day 03

Soul of the Volcano

Venture inland to the ancient ruins of Akrotiri, the "Minoan Pompeii". Later, visit a traditional winery built into the volcanic rock for a private tasting of the island's unique wines.

  • check_circle Akrotiri Guided Tour
  • check_circle Volcanic Vineyard Visit
  • check_circle 6-Course Wine Pairing
